About StrideSync Hockey

Revolutionizing hockey performance through data-driven biomechanics analysis

Our Mission

At StrideSync, we aim to challenge traditional methods compared to following normalized processes & systems. Hockey has long been behind the curve when it comes to using science, data, and research. We aim to shrink this gap for the purpose of creating elite impactful hockey players.

We take shape as a company like a consulting firm. This style prioritizes constant evaluation of our own leadership, employees, along with identifying how we can improve so keeping an eye on trends, new research, and much more. Like our clients, we're in pursuit of excellence and mastery.
